What You Get

Everything your team needs
to set and achieve goals

Built on a structured OKR framework — real-time collaboration, with an AI engine running quietly in the background at every step.

Real-Time Collaborative Sessions

Launch live OKR workshops where your team adds, edits, votes on, and comments on goals simultaneously — all visible in real-time via WebSocket.

AI-Generated Goals & Objectives

AI analyzes your organization's context and suggests relevant goals, structured objectives, and measurable key results — so you start every session with a head start.

Structured OKR Framework

A clear hierarchy: Goals → Objectives → Key Results. Each layer is quantifiable and linked, giving teams and managers a coherent view of strategy-to-execution.

Smart Weightage Allocation

Assign priority percentages to each objective. AI recommends optimal weightage distributions; participants can suggest their own — all summing to 100% with live validation.

Progress Tracking & Dashboards

Personal dashboards show your assigned goals, objectives, and key results with live progress. Managers see team-wide performance. Export dashboards as PDF with one click.

Goal Snapshots & Comparison Reports

Freeze goal progress at any point in time as a named snapshot. Compare multiple goals or snapshots side-by-side to identify trends and measure improvement over periods.

Data Request System

Admins can request key result progress data from team members on a schedule. Recipients submit values via a dedicated portal — even without logging in to the main app.

Role-Based Access Control

Four roles — Admin, Manager, Participant, and Viewer — with precisely scoped permissions. Admins lead sessions, participants suggest and vote, viewers observe in real-time.

The Process

From setup to insight
in six clear stages

One continuous line, six stages — scroll to move the pipeline forward.

01/06
portal.goalytics.io/onboarding
Stage 01

Set Up Your Organization

Admin completes onboarding: creates the organization, defines Team Types (Projects, Departments, Employees), and adds the people who will participate in sessions.

portal.goalytics.io/sessions/new
Stage 02

Launch a Session & Invite Your Team

Create a Collaborative Session for a project, department, or employee. Invite collaborators by email — assigning each as Participant or Viewer. Schedule the session with timezone support.

portal.goalytics.io/session/goals
Stage 03

Brainstorm Goals Together, Live

Everyone joins the live session. Team members add goals, like their favorites, and leave comments — all seen in real-time. AI suggests additional goals based on org context. Admin finalizes the goal list.

portal.goalytics.io/session/okrs
Stage 04

Define Objectives & Key Results

For each finalized goal, the team collaborates on objectives — the qualitative "what" — and key results — the quantitative "how much." Participants can suggest; admins confirm and finalize.

portal.goalytics.io/session/weightage
Stage 05

Set Objective Priorities (Weightage)

Assign percentage weights to each objective within a goal. Team members suggest their own weightages; AI recommends optimal distributions. Admin applies the final values ensuring they sum to 100%.

portal.goalytics.io/dashboard
Stage 06

Track, Submit Data & Compare

Progress is tracked on goal dashboards. Admins request data submissions from team members. Snapshots freeze progress at key moments. The Comparison Report shows trends across goals and time periods.

Document-Grounded Context

Used in: Organization & Team Type Setup

Goal Generation

AI analyzes your organization, team type, and historical context to generate a shortlist of relevant, realistic strategic goals before your session even starts.

Used in: Goal Setup

Objective Structuring

For each finalized goal, AI proposes a set of well-defined, measurable objectives — saving hours of structured thinking and prompting discussion among your team.

Used in: Collaborative Session

Key Result Proposals

AI drafts quantifiable key results — including metric, target value, unit, and baseline — so every objective has a clear success measure from day one.

Weightage Balancing

AI recommends optimal percentage distributions across your objectives, ensuring priorities are proportional to impact. Team can accept, modify, or apply its own values.
