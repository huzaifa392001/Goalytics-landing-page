The Problem We Solve

OKRs fail when goals
are set in isolation.

The Old Way

Leadership sets goals top-down. Teams are told to execute. No one fully understands the why. Engagement drops, alignment breaks, and goals become box-checking exercises by Q4.

  • Siloed goal-setting with zero team input
  • No shared context between leaders and executors
  • Quarterly reviews reveal misalignment too late

The Goalytics Way

Every session is a live collaborative workspace. Goals are built with your team — not handed down to them. The result is alignment that sticks, accountability that's intrinsic, and progress that's visible.

  • Real-time sessions where every voice is heard
  • AI ensures structure and completeness
  • Live dashboards keep everyone on the same page

The Process

From setup to insight
in six clear steps

01

Set Up Your Organization

Admin completes onboarding: creates the organization, defines Team Types (Projects, Departments, Employees), and adds the people who will participate in sessions.

02

Launch a Session & Invite Your Team

Create a Collaborative Session for a project, department, or employee. Invite collaborators by email — assigning each as Participant or Viewer. Schedule the session with timezone support.

03

Brainstorm Goals Together, Live

Everyone joins the live session. Team members add goals, like their favorites, and leave comments — all seen in real-time. AI suggests additional goals based on org context. Admin finalizes the goal list.

04

Define Objectives & Key Results

For each finalized goal, the team collaborates on objectives — the qualitative "what" — and key results — the quantitative "how much." Participants can suggest; admins confirm and finalize.

05

Set Objective Priorities (Weightage)

Assign percentage weights to each objective within a goal. Team members suggest their own weightages; AI recommends optimal distributions. Admin applies the final values ensuring they sum to 100%.

06

Track, Submit Data & Compare

Progress is tracked on goal dashboards. Admins request data submissions from team members. Snapshots freeze progress at key moments. The Comparison Report shows trends across goals and time periods.

AI Integration

AI isn't a feature.
It's your strategy partner.

AI is woven through every layer of Goalytics — from the first brainstorm to long-term performance analysis.

01

Document-Grounded Context

Upload strategy decks, briefs, or other org documents when creating an Organization or Team Type. AI ingests this context so every suggestion downstream is grounded in your real strategy — not generic templates.

Used in: Organization & Team Type Setup
02

Goal Generation

AI analyzes your organization, team type, and historical context to generate a shortlist of relevant, realistic strategic goals before your session even starts.

Used in: Goal Setup
03

Objective Structuring

For each finalized goal, AI proposes a set of well-defined, measurable objectives — saving hours of structured thinking and prompting discussion among your team.

Used in: Collaborative Session
04

Key Result Proposals

AI drafts quantifiable key results — including metric, target value, unit, and baseline — so every objective has a clear success measure from day one.

Used in: Key Result Setup
05

Weightage Balancing

AI recommends optimal percentage distributions across your objectives, ensuring priorities are proportional to impact. Team can accept, modify, or apply its own values.

Used in: Weightage Step
06

Dashboard Insights

On any goal dashboard, trigger AI analysis to get plain-language performance commentary — what's on track, what needs attention, and actionable recommendations.

Used in: Goal Dashboard
07

Agentic Mode

Enable fully autonomous AI sessions where AI proposes the complete OKR structure — goals, objectives, key results, and weightages — for your team to review, refine, and finalize.

Used in: Agentic Sessions
08

Snapshot Insights

When a goal snapshot is taken, AI provides an interpretive analysis comparing it to previous snapshots — surfacing trends and identifying risks before they become blockers.

Used in: Snapshots
09

Comparison Reports

Compare multiple goals side-by-side, or stack a goal's snapshots against each other over time — AI surfaces the trends and deltas so you can see exactly what's improving and what's stalling.

Used in: Comparison Reports
